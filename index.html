December feels warmer this year.

Two days. Side by side.

Somewhere between late nights and quiet mornings, baby boy learned how to care better.

And baby girl— with her calm strength— made it feel safe to stay.

I’m still a puppy sometimes. Learning. Trying. Showing up.

Love hurts but we power through. Not loudly. Just… together.

Tea cooling by the window. Simit crumbs on the table. Baklava sweetness lingering.

Istanbul evenings. The Bosphorus breathing slow. Lights reflecting—never still.

Some lyrics don’t need to be named. They sit between lines. Like a song we both hear differently— and still understand.

This is not a promise.

Just care, held gently. Always rooting for you.
